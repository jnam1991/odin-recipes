Vegan Eggnog

Description

Try a dairy-free twist on this classic holiday beverage. Blending plant-based milk, and warm spices, it offers a delightful alternative without compromising on the festive taste. Perfect for everyone to enjoy the seasonal spirit.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 15 ounce full fat coconut milk can
  • 2 cups plant-based milk
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons organic sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon vinilla extract

Optional Ingredients

  • about 1/2 cup of spiced rum
  • cinnamon sticks for garnish

Directions

  1. Shake the can of coconut milk vigorously to minimize chunks. Combine the coconut milk along with the rest of the Main Eggnog Ingredients in a small saucepan over medium heat. Cook, whisking frequently, until the spices mostly dissolve and the nog is totally smooth and heated through, for about 10-15 minutes. Dont let the mixture boil, because you dont want a skin to form on top.
  2. Divide your rum(if you're using it) between four to six mugs. Either way, top off your mug with eggnog from the pan. Garnish with a cinnamon stick if you want.
